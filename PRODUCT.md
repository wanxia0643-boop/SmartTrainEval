# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students complete software practical-training work and review feedback. Teachers evaluate submissions and manage cohorts. Enterprise mentors guide projects and surface talent evidence. Administrators configure the platform and monitor operations. They work primarily in focused desktop sessions where task status, evidence, and decisions must be immediately legible.

## Product Purpose

智训评 is a software practical-training intelligence and evaluation system. It unifies training projects, evaluation progress, capability evidence, feedback, and role-specific work queues so every participant can act on the next important task.

## Brand Personality

Clear, professional, and encouraging. It combines the calm structure of an education platform with the precision of a modern operational tool.

## Anti-references

Avoid decorative dashboards, excessive card grids, gradients, neon effects, glass surfaces, oversized metrics, and visually noisy gamification. The interface should never make a learning or evaluation task harder to scan.

## Design Principles

1. Make the next evaluation action unmistakable.
2. Let learning evidence and data explain progress at a glance.
3. Give every role a focused, relevant workspace.
4. Prefer familiar, dependable administration patterns over novelty.
5. Keep performance feedback constructive and easy to act upon.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA for keyboard access, visible focus, contrast, labelled controls, form errors, and reduced-motion support. Do not rely on color alone for task state.
